Frequently Asked questions

What is FTTH Technology?
  • Fiber to the home (FTTH), also called "fiber to the premises" (FTTP), is the installation and use of optical fiber from a central point directly to individual buildings such as residences, apartment buildings and businesses to provide unprecedented high-speed Internet access.
  • FTTH dramatically increases the connection speeds available to computer users compared with technologies now used in most places.
  • FTTH promises connection speeds of up to 100 megabits per second (Mbps) -- 20 to 100 times as fast as a typical cable modem or DSL (Digital Subscriber Line) connection.
What is my Account Number /User Id ?
  • Account number is a unique number assigned for your GTPL billing account.
  • You can check your account number on your GTPL statement.
I Have Forgotten My Selfcare Password, What Do I Do Now?
  • Go to https://selfcare.gtpl.net/Login.aspx
  • Click "Forgot Password".
  • Enter your email id which is registered for self-care account
  • The password will be sent to your registered mobile number and email address.
How Do I Log-In / Log-Out Using Wifi?
  • Connect the GTPL cable to the input jack on the Wi-Fi router.
  • You are required to configure the router as per the specifications of the make and model
  • Your GTPL user id and password will be have to be saved on the router configuration page.
  • Also, you will need to also give a router identifier name (SSID) and a router login password (SSID Pwd)
